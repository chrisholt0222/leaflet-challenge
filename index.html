Visualizing Data with Leaflet - Earthquakes


The United States Geological Survey (USGS) collects and provides scientific data on natural hazards and the health of our ecosystems and environment. Their scientists develop new methods and tools to supply timely, relevant, and useful information about the Earth and its processes. This project creates a map utilizing Leaflet showing earthquakes and the magnituge based on USGS data (geoJson).

Leaflet-Step-1

The first plot shows the all earthquakes for the day pulled from the USGS feed. The plot reflects the magnitude of the earthquake based on both the size and the color of each circle. Earthquakes with higher magnitudes appear larger and darker in color.

Leaflet-Step-2

The second plot includes additional information and additional map options. For each earthquake, a popup is available providing location, magnitude and date. The fault line boundaries can are added as well.
